About us

We are a society of puzzle enthusiasts from the University of Techonology, Sydney. Our interests include puzzle hunts, escape rooms, brain teasers, and anything that challenges the mind. Our goal is to create a community at UTS where everyone can come together and indulge in their passion for everything puzzles.
